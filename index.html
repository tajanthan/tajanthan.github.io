About Me

Since June 2017 I’m a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Torr Vision Group at University of Oxford. I obtained my PhD from the College of Engineering and Computer Science at the Australian National University in May 2017. During my PhD I was also a member of the Analytics group at Data61, CSIRO and I was primarily supervised by Prof. Richard Hartley. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Moratuwa.

Research Interests

Graphical Models, Optimization Algorithms and Machine Learning.
