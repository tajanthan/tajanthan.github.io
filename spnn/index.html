Signal Propagation in Neural Networks

Thalaiyasingam Ajanthan1, Namhoon Lee2, Kartik Gupta1, Stephen Gould1, and Philip H. S. Torr2

1 Australian National University, 2 University of Oxford

Abstract

Understanding the signal propagation properties in a neural network is not only essential for improving its trainability but it also sheds some light on the robustness properties of neural networks. In this project, we study the effects of signal propagation on both of these aspects especially on compressed (either sparse or quantized) networks.
