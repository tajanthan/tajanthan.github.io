Incremental Learning

Thalaiyasingam Ajanthan1, Arslan Chaudhry2, Puneet K. Dokania2, Marcus Rohrbach3, Mohamed Elhoseiny4, Marc’Aurelio Ranzato3, and Philip H. S. Torr2

1 Australian National University, 2 University of Oxford, 3 Facebook AI Research, 4 King Abdullah University of Science and Technology

Abstract

Learning from a stream of data without forgetting is important for lifelong learning systems. In this project, we study the incremental learning problem for a classification model, develop evaluation metrics and discuss strategies to mitigate catastrophic forgetting.
